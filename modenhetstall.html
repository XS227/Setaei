Maturity Number (Modenhetstall)

Blend the name and destiny frequencies the way Åse Steinsland teaches to uncover the life theme that blossoms with age and experience.

Why the maturity number matters

The maturity number synthesises who you are becoming. It forecasts the qualities, callings and milestones that ripen after midlife, showing how the name and birth path weave into a guiding destiny.

How we calculate Modenhetstall

  1. Calculate the name number (Navnetall) with Åse’s Pythagorean mapping or Abjad translation.
  2. Determine the destiny number (Skjebnetall) by reducing the birth date in the Western calendar.
  3. Add the two master vibrations together and reduce again, keeping 11, 22 and 33 intact.
  4. Interpret the resulting tone as the matured expression of your gifts, callings and legacy.
