The Origin Story

Meg Selfie began as a personal manifesto and grew into a global movement. With roots in architecture, creative coding, and community organizing, Khabat shapes spaces where technology is gentle, intuitive, and deeply human.

Instead of relying on images, Meg Selfie orchestrates light, typography, voice, and data storytelling to document identity in motion. Every installation is a bespoke, collaborative studio session where participants co-create the narrative.

Guiding Principles

  • Radical Empathy: Every interaction begins with listening and ends with shared authorship.
  • Sensory Minimalism: Typography, light, and language replace conventional photography to frame new perspectives.
  • Living Archives: Meg Selfie sessions become evolving constellations of community wisdom.
  • Ethical Innovation: Privacy-respecting technology and transparent storytelling are non-negotiable.
