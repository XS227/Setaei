Why the soul urge number matters

The soul urge (heart’s desire) number highlights the passions, longings and private values that animate a person beyond public roles. By isolating vowel tones, Åse’s method tunes into the breath of the name—the whispers of what feels most fulfilling.

How we calculate Hjertetall

  1. Normalise the name to Western letters, replacing Æ with AE, Ø with OE and Å with AA while keeping master vowels intact.
  2. Extract vowels (A, E, I, O, U, Y) and vowel sounds from right-to-left scripts using the Abjad equivalents before assigning Åse’s classic 1–9 values.
  3. Add the vowel values together and reduce them to a single digit, unless the sum is 11, 22 or 33—master vibrations remain whole.
  4. Interpret the final frequency to understand what brings joy, devotion and inner satisfaction.
