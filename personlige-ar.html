Personal Year (Personlige år)

Track the annual rhythm Åse Steinsland follows by combining birth month and day with the current year—always presented in Western numerals for clarity.

Why the personal year matters

Each personal year signals a season of focus. From planting seeds to harvesting achievements, the nine-year cycle helps you align goals and expectations with the energy at hand.

How we calculate Personlige år

  1. Write the birth month and day as Western digits (we automatically convert non-Gregorian dates beforehand).
  2. Add these digits to the digits of the target calendar year.
  3. Reduce the sum to a single digit, keeping 11 and 22 as master year influences.
  4. Interpret the resulting number to plan actions, self-care and timing.
