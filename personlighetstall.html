Personality Number (Personlighetstall)

Åse Steinsland’s consonant-focused formula reveals the first impression, style and protective armour a name presents to the world.

Why the personality number matters

The personality number shapes how others perceive you before they know your inner story. By translating consonant rhythm into digits, we spotlight the traits, aesthetics and boundaries that influence social encounters.

How we calculate Personlighetstall

  1. Normalise the name into Åse’s Western alphabet, honouring special Norwegian letters and Abjad consonants from right-to-left scripts.
  2. Collect consonants (including Y when it sounds like a consonant) and map them to the classic 1–9 scale.
  3. Add the consonant values together, reducing the total until a single digit remains unless the sum is a master number 11, 22 or 33.
  4. Interpret the resulting frequency to understand how the world experiences the name at first glance.
